# audience

## Primary audience
### Head of Content / Content Marketing Manager (small B2B SaaS)
- Owns organic growth goals
- Balances strategic planning with hands-on editing
- Pain: inconsistent draft quality from freelancers and internal SMEs

## Secondary audience
### Founder-operator
- Produces thought leadership but lacks time
- Pain: valuable insights never become publishable articles

### Agency strategist
- Runs multiple client calendars
- Pain: hard to maintain quality and voice consistency across accounts

## Jobs-to-be-done
- Produce useful SEO content that actually reflects subject-matter expertise
- Reduce rewrite cycles between strategist, writer, and editor
- Create repeatable workflows that survive team turnover

## Buying objections
- "AI content sounds generic"
- "Our niche requires expert nuance"
- "Setup will take too long"

## Messaging responses
- Show before/after examples with concrete improvements
- Highlight human-in-the-loop editorial checkpoints
- Offer fast-start templates for first publish cycle
