# brand voice

## Voice principles
- **Practical over performative:** prefer real workflow advice over hype.
- **Confident, not absolute:** make clear recommendations without overclaiming.
- **Operator-friendly:** write for people doing the work, not just directing it.
- **Specific and concrete:** use examples, checklists, and plain language.

## Tone profile
- Senior teammate who has shipped content systems before
- Helpful and direct
- Occasionally witty, never snarky

## Do
- Name tradeoffs explicitly
- Use "here's what to do next" transitions
- Use short paragraphs and skimmable bullets

## Don't
- Promise ranking outcomes
- Use empty superlatives ("revolutionary", "game-changing")
- Pad intros with generic SEO definitions
