# products

## Company snapshot
- **Brand:** LanternLoop
- **Category:** B2B SaaS for founder-led content teams
- **Core promise:** Turn expert knowledge trapped in calls, Slack threads, and docs into publish-ready SEO drafts and repurposable content assets.

## Primary product
### LanternLoop Studio
A workflow product that helps small marketing teams:
1. Capture source material (call transcripts, notes, docs)
2. Build keyword-aligned briefs
3. Generate structured first drafts with citations to source docs
4. Route draft through editorial checks before publishing

## Key capabilities
- Source-library ingestion (docs + transcripts)
- Brief builder with intent framing
- Outline and draft generation
- Editorial QA checklists
- Content refresh recommendations for aging articles

## Ideal customer profile
- SaaS companies with lean marketing teams
- Agencies managing multi-client content calendars
- Founder-led businesses without dedicated SEO specialists

## Product boundaries
- Not a replacement for final human editorial approval
- Does not guarantee rankings
- Requires customer-provided source material for factual accuracy
