# seo guidelines

## On-page standards
- One clear primary keyword and 3-8 semantically related terms
- Title should communicate audience + outcome, not just topic
- Intro should satisfy likely query intent within first 120 words
- Include an FAQ only if it resolves real objections or next-step questions

## Topical strategy
- Prioritize BOFU/MOFU keywords tied to workflow pain points
- Build clusters around repeatable systems, templates, and implementation guides
- Avoid chasing broad vanity keywords without business alignment

## Internal linking approach
- Link from educational posts to product-relevant workflow pages
- Use descriptive anchor text tied to user tasks
- Include at least 3 meaningful internal link opportunities per article

## Measurement mindset
- Optimize for qualified traffic and conversion path clarity, not just sessions
